Ph.D. Candidate, Computer Science · Clemson University

Parisha Joshi

I build data-driven models that recover physical properties, like light spectra and material behavior, from limited or noisy measurements, combining classical optimization with neural methods. My PhD work applies this to inverse rendering and structural color, to solve the underlying problem of inferring hidden physical parameters from sparse data across vision, sensing, and simulation.
